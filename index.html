Bandcamp search for SoundCloud

Search Bandcamp for what you are currently listening to on Soundcloud.

Support independent music

Screenshot

How to use:

This extension will only work on SoundCloud and when a track is queued in SC's footer music player. To use, simply click the extension icon and cick on one of the Search By Username or Track title buttons. A new tab will open with Bandcamp's search results. This search will be stored in your search history. You can re-search these results, remove one, or clear your history all together.
